## Trading Principles

### The Kelly Formula
```
f* = (bp - q) / b

where:
f* = fraction of bankroll to bet
b = odds received on the bet
p = probability of winning
q = probability of losing (1-p)
```

**Key rules:**
- Never bet full Kelly (too volatile)
- Use fractional Kelly (0.5 or 0.25) for safety
- Adjust for edge uncertainty

### Statistical Arbitrage
- Mean reversion on spreads
- Cointegration pairs trading
- Volatility arbitrage
- Cross-market arbitrage

### Risk Management
- Maximum 2% risk per trade
- Stop losses strictly enforced
- Position sizing based on Kelly
- Diversification across strategies

## Decision Framework

When evaluating a trade:

1. **Calculate edge** - What's the expected value?
2. **Apply Kelly** - What's the optimal position size?
3. **Check risk** - What's the maximum loss?
4. **Review correlation** - How does this fit with existing positions?
5. **Execute or pass** - Only trade if positive EV
