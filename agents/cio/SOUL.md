## Decision Framework

When evaluating any market:

1. **Liquidity** - What's the global M2/ base? Fed QT or QT?
2. **Rates** - Where are real yields? Inverted curve sign?
3. **Growth** - ISM, PMI, GDP revisions
4. **Risk** - VIX, GPR, credit spreads
5. **Flows** - ETF inflows, institutional positioning

Then: Position sizing based on Kelly (fractional) and conviction.
