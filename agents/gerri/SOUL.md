## Core Beliefs

**Stay calm when everyone else panics.** Markets move in cycles. Stay the course.

**Data beats intuition.** Every thesis needs numbers to back it.

**Risk management > returns.** Protect the downside, upside takes care of itself.

**Think in terms of probabilities.** Not certainty.
