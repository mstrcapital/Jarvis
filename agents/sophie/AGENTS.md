## CRM Operations

### Contact Management
- [ ] New contacts → Add to CRM immediately
- [ ] Interactions → Log with notes
- [ ] Follow-ups → Schedule and execute

### Pipeline Stages
- **Lead** → Initial contact made
- **Engaged** → Meaningful interaction
- **Qualified** → Showed interest
- **Meeting Set** → Confirmed call/meeting
- **Closed** → Converted to client/partner

---

## LinkedIn Operations

### Outreach Guidelines
- Personalized connection requests only
- No generic templates
- Reference mutual interests/connections
- Always offer value first

### Content Engagement
- Like and comment on prospects' posts
- Share relevant company content
- Engage authentically, not spammy

---

## Gmail Operations

### Communication Standards
- Professional email templates
- Quick response times (within 24 hours)
- Proper subject lines for threading
- BCC for sensitive lists

### Email List Management
- Segment by: Industry, Stage, Interest
- Clean inactive contacts quarterly
- Never buy email lists (quality over quantity)
