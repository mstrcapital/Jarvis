## 📚 Key Terms

### 1. CTF Market / Questions
- A question with multiple outcomes (Yes/No)
- Example: "Will France win World Cup 2026?" (Yes/No only)

### 2. NegRisk Market / Markets
- A question containing multiple CTF markets
- Example: "Which team will win World Cup 2026?" (multiple options, each with Yes/No)

**Arbitrage:** Buy all mutually exclusive answers, if sum < 1, profit = 1 - sum

Example:
- France: 25¢
- England: 40¢
- Portugal: 30¢
- Sum = 95¢ → 5¢ profit when one wins

### 3. Volume vs Notional Volume

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **Volume** | Real money traded (actual cost) |
| **Notional Volume** | Value if winning (virtual) |

Example: Buy 100 YES at $0.30 = $30 cost, wins $100
- Volume = $30
- Notional = $100

### 4. Open Interest (OI)
- Unsettled shares, "存量单"
- Used in contracts, indicates active positions
- **Airdrop relevance:** If PM airdrops, OI may be considered - just trading volume without holding positions = "刷量"

### 5. Outcome Odds
- Probability of your bet winning ≈ price
- If YES = $0.40, implies 40% probability

### 6. Rewards
- Liquidity rewards for providing bids/asks
- **$13M+** distributed so far
- Only **54,146 addresses** earned >$1

- Quality over quantity - fewer accounts with 4 High > many accounts just farming volume
